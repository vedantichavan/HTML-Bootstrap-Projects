About Us

From the vibrant spices to the diverse range of dishes, each bite at Kerala Hut transports you to the lush landscapes and warm hospitality of Kerala.

We bring the flavors and traditions of Kerala, India to your table. Nestled in the heart of Pune, our cafe offers an authentic taste of Kerala's rich culinary heritage. Our passionate chefs meticulously craft each dish using traditional recipes passed down through generations, ensuring an unforgettable dining experience. Whether you're craving the tangy flavors of fish curry, the comforting warmth of appam and stew, or the irresistible sweetness of payasam, our menu has something to delight every palate. Come join us at Kerala Hut and embark on a culinary journey through the flavors of Kerala.
